Frequently Asked Questions

❓ How do I apply for admission?

You can fill out the online application form on our Admissions page or visit the school to collect a physical form.

❓ When does the academic session begin?

The academic year starts in September. However, we accept mid-term transfers based on available spaces.

❓ Is there a scholarship program?

Yes, we offer merit-based and need-based scholarships. Contact our admissions office for details.

❓ What after-school activities are available?

We have sports, STEM club, debate, arts, music, and coding classes among others.
