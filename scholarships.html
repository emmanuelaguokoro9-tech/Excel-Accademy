Frequently Asked Questions

📅 When is the scholarship deadline?

The application deadline is May 31st, 2026 for the upcoming academic session.

💰 Is the scholarship renewable?

Yes, scholarships are reviewed annually based on academic performance and conduct. Scholars must maintain a minimum 70% average.

📝 Is there an entrance exam?

Yes, shortlisted candidates will take a scholarship examination and interview.

🎯 Can international students apply?

Yes, scholarships are open to all students eligible for admission to Excel Academy.

📄 What documents are required?

Last report card, birth certificate, parent ID, and recommendation letter.

⏰ How long does processing take?

Applications are reviewed within 2-3 weeks after the deadline.
