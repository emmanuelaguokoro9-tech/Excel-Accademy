Welcome to Excel Academy

Dear Student, welcome to Excel Academy! You are now part of a community committed to excellence, discipline, and integrity. This handbook is your companion throughout your journey here. It outlines the expectations, opportunities, and support systems available to help you succeed academically and personally.

🌟 Our Promise to You

We promise to provide a safe, nurturing, and challenging environment where you can discover your potential, build lasting friendships, and develop into a responsible global citizen.

Our Mission & Vision

Vision

To become a center of educational excellence producing globally competitive and responsible citizens.

Mission

To empower students with knowledge, skills, and values to thrive in a changing world while maintaining academic excellence and moral discipline.

📖 Core Values

Discipline • Excellence • Integrity • Service — These values guide everything we do at Excel Academy.

Code of Conduct

Every student is expected to uphold the highest standards of behavior. Respect yourself, respect others, and respect the school environment.

Golden Rules
  • Be punctual to all classes and school activities
  • Show respect to teachers, staff, and fellow students
  • No bullying, fighting, or any form of harassment
  • Mobile phones are strictly prohibited during school hours
  • Keep the school clean and litter-free
  • Complete all assignments and homework on time

Disciplinary Consequences: Violations may result in detention, parental meeting, suspension, or expulsion depending on severity.

Academic Policies

Excel Academy follows a rigorous curriculum aligned with the Nigerian National Curriculum, preparing students for WAEC, NECO, and beyond.

📊 Assessment Structure

Continuous Assessment (CA): 40% (includes tests, assignments, projects, participation)
Examinations: 60% (end of term exams)
Termly grading is cumulative.

🏆 Promotion Criteria

Students must achieve a minimum 50% average to be promoted. SS3 students must meet WAEC/NECO registration requirements.

Attendance & Punctuality

Regular attendance is essential for academic success. School hours: 8:00 AM – 3:00 PM (Monday–Friday). Morning assembly at 7:45 AM.

  • Late arrival after 8:00 AM is recorded as a tardy
  • Absences require a written excuse from parent/guardian
  • Medical leave: doctor's note required for absences over 3 days
  • Students with below 80% attendance may be disqualified from examinations

Dress Code & Uniform

All students must wear the approved school uniform during school hours and official events.

Health & Safety

Your safety is our priority. Excel Academy maintains comprehensive safety protocols.

  • On-site clinic with registered nurse during school hours
  • 24/7 CCTV surveillance across campus
  • Regular fire drills and safety training
  • Counseling services available for students
  • All visitors must check in at security post

🏥 In Case of Emergency

Parents will be contacted immediately. Keep emergency contact information current with the school office.
